Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers!

We understand that choosing the right school for your child is important. Here are answers to some common questions about our Montessori programs, safety, and admissions.

What age groups do you admit?

We admit children from 2 years to 6 years of age. Our programs include Day Care, Pre-School, and LKG with age-appropriate Montessori activities.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Our campus is safe, secure, and child-friendly. We maintain clean classrooms, child-safe furniture, regular sanitization, and constant supervision by trained staff.

How do you keep parents updated on their child's progress?

We conduct regular parent-teacher meetings and provide updates on your child's academic and developmental progress throughout the year.

Do you offer part-time or flexible scheduling?

Yes, we offer Day Care and flexible timing options to support working parents and different family needs.

What is your learning approach?

We follow the Montessori method, focusing on hands-on learning, independence, creativity, and holistic child development in a joyful and nurturing environment.

A joyful Montessori environment where little minds grow, explore, and shine every day.
